Lasagna recipe

Ingredients

  • For the Meat Sauce:

    • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or Italian sausage
    • 1 small onion, finely chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
    • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
    • 1 can (14 oz) tomato sauce
    • 1/4 cup red wine (optional)
    • 1 tsp dried basil
    • 1 tsp dried oregano
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 1/2 tsp black pepper
    • 1/2 tsp sugar (optional, helps cut acidity)

  • For the Cheese Mixture:

    • 15 oz ricotta cheese
    • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese (divided)
    • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 1 large egg
    • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley (or 1 tbsp dried)

  • For the Lasagna:

    • 12 lasagna noodles (regular or no-boil)
    • Olive oil for greasing the baking dish

Process:

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce:

    • Heat a large skillet or pan over medium heat. Add the ground beef (or sausage) and cook until browned, breaking it up into smaller pieces with a spoon.
    • Add the chopped onion and garlic, and cook for another 2-3 minutes until softened and fragrant.
    • Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and red wine (if using). Add the basil, oregano, salt, pepper, and sugar. Bring to a simmer and cook for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens.

  2. Cook the Lasagna Noodles (if using regular noodles):

    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water to prevent them from sticking. Lay them flat on a sheet of parchment or wax paper to keep them from sticking together.

  3. Prepare the Cheese Mixture:

    • In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, 1 1/2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, egg, and parsley. Mix until smooth and well combined.

  4. Assemble the Lasagna:

    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish with olive oil.
    • Start by spreading a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom of the dish.
    • Place a layer of lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
    • Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
    • Add a layer of meat sauce on top of the ricotta.
    • Repeat the layers: noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce.
    • Finish with a final layer of noodles, remaining meat sauce, and top with the remaining 1/2 cup of mozzarella cheese.

  5. Bake:

    • Cover the lasagna with aluminum foil (to prevent it from drying out) and bake for 25 minutes.
    • After 25 minutes, remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, until the cheese is golden and bubbly.

  6. Cool and Serve:

    • Let the lasagna cool for about 10-15 minutes before cutting into it, as it will hold its shape better.
    • Serve with a side salad or garlic bread for a complete meal!

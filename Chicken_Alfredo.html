Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 lb fettuccine pasta
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Process:

  1. Cook the Chicken:

    • Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook the chicken for 6-7 minutes per side until cooked through and golden brown. Remove from the skillet and let it rest for a few minutes. Slice into thin strips or bite-sized pieces.

  2. Cook the Fettuccine:

    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the fettuccine according to the package directions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  3. Make the Alfredo Sauce:

    • In the same skillet used to cook the chicken, melt butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
    • Add the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Reduce heat and cook for 3-4 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly. Stir in Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy. Season with salt and pepper.

  4. Combine:

    • Add the cooked fettuccine to the Alfredo sauce and toss to coat. If the sauce is too thick, add a little pasta water to reach your desired consistency.

  5. Serve

    • Top the pasta with sliced chicken, and garnish with chopped parsley and more Parmesan cheese if desired.
